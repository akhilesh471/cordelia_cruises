Pristine beaches, awe-inspiring seascapes with a thriving ecosystem, Lakshadweep is a destination that outshines its reputation. A beauty waiting to be explored, the name is derived from Sanskrit, signifying, ‘one hundred thousand islands’. The archipelago of Lakshadweep lies 300 miles west of Kerala. Only 10 out of the 36 islands here are inhabited and promote water-based, responsible tourism.
